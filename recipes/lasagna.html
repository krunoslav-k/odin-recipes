Lasagna

Description

A tasty Italian dish that combines lasagna sheets with delicous bolognese and holanede sauces.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced beef meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Milk
  • White flour
  • Spices
  • Cheese

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Prepare bolognese sauce
  2. Prepare holandese sauce
  3. Put sheets at the bottom of a pan, and then alternatly put the sauces on the lasagna sheets
  4. Cover the last lasagna sheet with cheese
  5. Put the pan with lasagna in the over for 35 mins and 200C
